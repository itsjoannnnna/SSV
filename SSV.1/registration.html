Registration



How to Participate?

  • Students must be over 18 years old, attend a recognized institution, and a desire to improve English proficiency in communication.
  • Program cost is tentatively $4,000 per participant and $1500 for chaperones. Fees include tuition, board, meals, local transportation, field trips, and cultural trips.
  • Participants will stay in SJSU dormitories with double-room, or triple-room accommodations.
  • Participants must cover their own air fare and health insurance costs.
  • International participants should seek advice from US Consolate, and for additional information please refer to U.S. Visas.
  • Participants or sponsored universities must sign agreements with SJSU for terms and conditions prior to registration.
  • The full payment is due on April 15th, 2016.
  • The program registration is refundable only if the participant cannot obtain their visa to enter the United States, and the request for refund must be completed by May 15, 2016.
  • No refunds will be issued after May 30, 2016.

Application is open for the Summer 2017 Program.
