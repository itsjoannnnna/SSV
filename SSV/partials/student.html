Student Mentors



Job Description

  • Participate in a 3-week (end of July) summer program in the Silicon Valley.
  • Mentor international students on engineering related projects.
  • Chaperone field trips, cultural trips, and company tours.
  • Deliver oral announcements and introductions for speakers.
  • Approximately 20 hours of work per week and possible weekend work is included.

Benefits

  • Work on-campus.
  • Network with engineering peers and international students.
  • All program activities are included (i.e. cultural trips, field trips, etc).

Minimum Requirements

  • Leadership abilities/takes initiative (Club/Organization officers).
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
  • GPA: 2.5 or higher.
  • Engineering majors (Junior or Senior standing).

Preferred Requirements

  • Possibility of working weekends.
  • Lives close to campus.
