Written by: Dai, Yuli
Major: Accounting
University: Chung Yuan Christian University, Taiwan

SSV is really worth to experiencing. I participated in the SSV with people from other places last summer and it was really an experience worth remembering.I had a deep visiting in many well-known companies in silicon valley like Intel, Cisco,EAG and so on. And many speeches about enterprising and innovation are also educational. Additionally, what impressed me most is the process to design and launch an IT product with teammates from different countries with diverse backgrounds. Take my team as an example, our teammates are from China, Taiwan and Saudi Arabia and some major in business while others major in engineering and information technology. In three weeks, we had to cooperate with each other and unit various knowledge to generate an innovative product and present it to public with powerpoint. What we created was a smart bracelet that can detect emotions and do relative reactions to make people happy. In the period, communicating was a big problem for us because none of us was English native speaker and we didn’t know other’s language at all.But it really engaged our ability to express ourselves clearly in patient in creative ways not just language. Of course, I learnt exotic culture and tradition a lot.When we fulfilled the goal eventually, I felt proud and moved for myself and my team. Can you imagine that we were just strangers three weeks ago and after these weeks we worked out a product together! So amazing to meet these guys to learn and create together in SSV! I recommend to take part in SSV and you will have a wonderful summer.

Written by: Yang, Ruei-Hong (Ian)
Major: Electrical Engineering
University: Feng Chia University, Taiwan

SSV is a pretty good summer camp for university students. It plans many wonderful speeches and invites many professional speakers to talk about Silicon Valley. You can learn a lot from these successful case and understand what Silicon Valley is. Speakers will share many kinds of information for different area and practical knowledge. So you don’t need to worry about that you can’t understand what this speech talk about. You can also make friends with other students from different country, get more knowledge from different culture and do a teamwork project with them. You need to use your professional skill to help your teammate to finish the project, learn how to solve the problem and try your best to come up with some good idea. That will train your creativity ability, problem-solve skill and learn how to be a team. The most interesting aspect is that SSV will take you to many famous place for exciting field trips. At last, just come to SSV summer camp for fun!
